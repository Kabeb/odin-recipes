Fried Egg

This is a recipe for a fried egg.

Ingredients

  • Egg
  • Black Pepper
  • Salt
  • Oil

Steps

  1. Heat pan to medium high and add a table spoon of oil
  2. Crack egg into pan
  3. Season with salt and pepper
  4. Cook until desired egg type
  5. Remove egg from pan and enjoy
