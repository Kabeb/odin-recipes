Peanut Butter and Jelly Sandwich

This is a recipe for a classic peanut butter and jelly sandwich

Ingredients

  • White bread
  • Peanut butter
  • Raspberry preserves
  • Chips

Steps

  1. Lay peanut butter on one side of both slices of bread
  2. lay a layer of Raspberry preserves on top of on half of peanut butter
  3. Add chips for an extra bit of flavor and crunch
  4. Enjoy!
