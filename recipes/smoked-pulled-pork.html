Smoked pulled Pork

This is a recipe for smoked pulled pork. It will be super moist and tasty with a simple recipe and long cook time.

Ingredients

  • Pork shoulder
  • Black Pepper
  • Salt
  • Apple cider

Steps

  1. Rub pork shoulder with even parts black pepper and salt. Nice thick coat will bring out the flavors and make a nice crust in the end.
  2. Lay prepared pork shoulder in the middle of the smoker at 225 degrees
  3. Cook until internal temp is 200 degrees. About 8 hours
  4. Remove pulled pork and let cool for 30 minutes, then pull apart with forks
  5. Serve and enjoy!
